## Reading Groups

We organize bi-weekly reading groups to present and discuss classic or seminal papers on various topics in economics. Each session is led by a member who presents a paper and facilitates group discussion.

**Format:**
- Bi-weekly sessions
- One presenter per session
- Open discussion following the presentation
- Papers shared in advance for preparation

**Topics span across economics, including:**
- Microeconomics & Game Theory
- Macroeconomics & Monetary Economics
- Development Economics
- Labor Economics
- Econometrics & Causal Inference
- Trade & International Economics
- Public Economics & Political Economy
